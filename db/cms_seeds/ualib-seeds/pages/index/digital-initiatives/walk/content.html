Web Archives for Longitudinal Knowledge

Spearheaded by the University of Waterloo, York University, and the University of Alberta, and with infrastructure provided by a Compute Canada Research Platforms and Portals grant, this project brings together interested Canadian partners to provide access to collections of Canadian websites crawled and archived by the Internet Archive.
