Winspear Business Library

The Winspear Business Library is the primary information service library for the Alberta School of Business at the University of Alberta. The library includes specialized databases, business reserve and student study space.

Instructions for Accessing Winspear Business Library

Access to Winspear Business Library

Located on the main floor of the Alberta School of Business building on the University of Alberta campus.

Closest Parking/Transit Options

  • LRT stop is University Station.
  • Bus stop is University Transit Centre.
  • Closest public parking is U Lot near Fine Arts or along Saskatchewan Drive. N. Check Parking Services for more parking information

Directions to/from above including details about wheelchair accessible entrances and pathways, availability of ramps and automatic door openers, etc

From the University Station & University Transit Centre, proceed North beside the West side of Rutherford Library. Winspear Library is on the main floor of the School of Business building.

Entrance to wheelchair ramp is at the front door. No elevators to mezzanine.

Building and Library Entrance(s)

Enter the School of Business through the south doors (main doors). Winspear Library is at the right end of the hallway.

Elevators

Enter the School of Business, turn right. Access to the 5th floor: Contact the General Office on the 3rd floor if the elevator does not reach the 5th floor.

Tables/Workstations

We have 1 large height-adjustable worktable. Stacking chairs are not height-adjustable but steno chairs are. There are 6 large regular tables & 4 wheelchair accessible group study rooms for current UA Business students only: Q (MBA), R, S, & E. Book from this link: https://apps.business.ualberta.ca/roombooker/

Washrooms

From the library front door, make the first right towards the glass doors. Washrooms are before the doors.

1 pull alarm at East end of library.
