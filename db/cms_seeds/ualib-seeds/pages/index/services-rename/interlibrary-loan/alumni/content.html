Interlibrary Loan for Alumni/Visitors

Alumni or visitors must first set-up a library account, before coming to the Interlibrary Loan and Document Delivery department to register for our service. For details on setting up a library account:

  • Alumni
  • Visitors

When registering for interlibrary loan service, you must come in person to our office with your valid U of A Library card (Alumni ONEcard or External Borrower card) to set up your billing information. If it's not possible to come in person, please contact our office.

Cost of Service (Filled Requests):

  • Alumni - $5.00
  • Visitors - $35.00

Please note: Searches for Alumni and Visitors are limited to North American locations.

Before submitting a request, check that we don't hold it at the UofA or in NEOS, or ask for assistance in locating materials in our collections.
