Literary Encyclopedia

Click here for access

Covers world literature. Includes biographies of major and minor writers, scholarly descriptions of texts written by these authors, and a variety of descriptive and critical essays on literary, cultural and historical matters to provide an understanding of the social contexts in which this writing was produced.
