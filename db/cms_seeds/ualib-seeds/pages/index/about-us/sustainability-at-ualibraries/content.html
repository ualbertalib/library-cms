Earth Day Tips for In the Office:

  • Turn off lights when not in the office (ie. at meetings/lunch break)
  • Reduce paper printing
  • Use a reusable cup for coffee/drinks
  • Reduce garbage and recycle at least 1 item in the day
  • Ride your bike to work day!
  • Place a drop off box in the office for donation of lightly used clothes that can be recycled at Goodwill
  • Save water and don't run the dishwasher
