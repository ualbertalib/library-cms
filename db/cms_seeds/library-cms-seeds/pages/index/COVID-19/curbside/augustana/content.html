Curbside Pickup at Augustana Library
Curbside Pickup FAQs

Step 1:

Find your items & place a hold

Place a hold in our library catalogue for items not available via HathiTrust. Select Augustana as the pickup location.

Watch our Augustana curbside pickup video!

Step 2:

Wait until you receive an email

Watch for a ‘holds available’ email prompting you to come to Augustana campus and pick up your holds from a designated location just outside of the Augustana Library, in the Forum -- within 10 days.

Step 2a:

Augustana staff and faculty are reminded that they must follow protocols as listed by the Augustana Dean and submit On Campus Visit Requests (link restricted to Augustana staff only) to come to campus to pick up their library materials. Non-residence students will need to check in at station placed at the entryway to the Forum Building to come to pick up their library materials.

Step 3:

Retrieve your holds

Your holds will be placed on a designated table just outside the Augustana Library. Items have been signed out to your library account.
