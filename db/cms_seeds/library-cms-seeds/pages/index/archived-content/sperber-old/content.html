About the Library

Named after Geoffery + Robyn Sperber, the Sperber Health Sciences Library provides collections and services in support of the research and teaching of the faculties in the College of Health Sciences: Nursing, Medicine + Dentistry, Pharmacy + Pharmacutical Sciences and Kinesiology, Sport + Recreation. Featuring an exhibit display of the Dentistry Museum Collection. Sperber Library also accommodates various learning and collaboration needs.
