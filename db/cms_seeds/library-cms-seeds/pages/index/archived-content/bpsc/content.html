BPSC Bruce Peel Special Collections

Bruce Peel Special Collections houses a world-class collection of rare books and archival materials treating a wide range of subjects.
The BPSC Website offers detailed information about rare research collections, exhibitions and Peel Workshops.

REGULAR EXHIBITION HOURS (September through March)

  • Mondays 1–4pm: Reserved for pre-arranged group visits*
  • Tuesdays: Closed
  • Wednesdays–Fridays 1–4pm: Open for drop-in visitors

REGULAR RESEARCH HOURS (September through March)

  • Mondays: No research appointments
  • Tuesdays 1–4pm: Reserved for quiet research, by appointment*
  • Wednesdays–Fridays 1–4pm: By appointment*

Note: Research appointments during the regular academic year, September-March, are booked on a first-come, first-served basis. Standard reading room policies and protocols, designed to protect rare materials, are in force. Please email bpsc@ualberta.ca for all group visits and research appointment inquiries. More information about our hours and services can be viewed here.

*To request a research appointment or a group exhibition visit please write to us at bpsc@ualberta.ca
