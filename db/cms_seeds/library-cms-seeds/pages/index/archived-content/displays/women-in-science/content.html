Women in Science Display

Located in the entrance of the Science and Technology (Cameron) Library - January / February 2020

The University of Alberta Library is pleased to present the Women in Science display at the Cameron Library. The display highlights current UofA women scientists as well as famous female scientists from around the world. Thank you to our current UofA researchers for agreeing to be a part of this display.

Woman in Science Statistics

The statistics in the Pie Chart poster come from between 2009 and 2011 and were mainly gathered from Statistics Canada. The Why so Few? / Why Does it Matter? poster represents information from a variety of sources about women in science (references below). If you have specific questions about these statistics please contact the Science and Technology Library.
