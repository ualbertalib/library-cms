HUMAN RESOURCES: Work Related Injury or Illness (WCB)

{{cms:snippet staffhub-hr-sidenav}}

Injury Reporting

Should an incident take place where an employee has a work related injury or illness, the staff member must notify their supervisor and LibHR must be contacted as soon as possible. The employee and employer must fill out Workers Compensation Board reports (see below) within 72 hours of the injury or illness.

  • WCB - Employer Report of Injury LibHR will work with the supervisor to fill out employer report.
  • WCB - Worker Report of Injury or Occupational Disease
  • Incident Report
  • Work Related Injury or Illness - Information for Staff
  • Work Related Injury or Illness - Information for Managers

If you have ergonomic concerns follow the steps outlined on this webpage in order to assess your needs and determine next steps.
