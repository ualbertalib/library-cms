Library Services for Fall 2020

Print Collections / Physical Collections

Library materials will continue to be available to request for curbside pickup.

As of September 1st, UA students and staff will be able place requests on available items from participating NEOS Libraries for pickup at Rutherford Library. Some restrictions will apply. NEOS students & staff from participating libraries will be able to request UA items for pick up at their home locations.

As of September 14th, the Library’s Curbside Service will have a new and improved experience. The new service will have longer hours, more spaces, and you can pick up your item at any point during our Curbside Service hours on the date you booked.

Curbside pick-up is now available at Augustana Library.

Online Library Collections

Online collections will continue to be available from the library website for current UA Students and staff.

Current UA Students and staff will continue to be able to access library resources from off-campus. including:

  • Guides to Library Resources By Subject
  • Course Materials (online only)
    • Tools for Instructors: Create Reading Lists and Include them in eClass
  • Inter-Library Loan service will continue with delivery in digital format to the extent possible.

Library Locations

New! We will have a new seat booking/check-in system in Cameron Library for current UA staff and students so you can feel comfortable that you are a safe distance away from other library patrons. Our tentative release date for this service is September 1st, 2020.

As of August 26, Cameron Library hours are: Mon-Fri 8am-6pm, Sat 11am-6pm (starting September 5), closed Sun & Stat Holidays

Note: We are closed on Monday, August 31, 2020

All other Library Locations will be closed until further notice.

Online Services

We will continue to be available through our Ask Us! and Chat services.

As of August 31, chat hours will be: Mon-Thurs 8am-8pm, Fri 8am-6pm, Sat & Sun 11am-6pm, Closed Stat Holidays.

As always you will be able to email us anytime, and we will respond within 2 business days.

Librarians will continue to be available for instruction, consultations, etc.
