Rules & Information

Only current UA students/staff can book up two (2) sessions per day; each session up to a maximum of 4 hours per session. If you need to reserve more than 48 hours ahead of time, please contact us.

Please be aware that this is a public shared use space. Access to Cameron Library is limited to current UA students and staff only

To ensure safe and shared use of the space, a seat booking is required for entry into Cameron Library. Only seating for individual study is available. No group work is permitted.

Effective July 1, 2021 masks are strongly encouraged but no longer required in Cameron Library.

Food is not allowed. Beverages in closed containers are permitted.

All computer workstations are connected to ONEcard printers (ONEprint). Windows Quickprint stations meant for short-term use limited to printing only.

Cleaning will take place throughout the day. We recommend that you bring cleaning supplies for immediate cleaning of computers and surfaces.

We will hold your seat for 30 minutes. If you are not in your seat within 30 minutes of the start of your booking, you will forfeit your booking and have to rebook.

Please make sure you check-out as soon as you are finished with your seat.
