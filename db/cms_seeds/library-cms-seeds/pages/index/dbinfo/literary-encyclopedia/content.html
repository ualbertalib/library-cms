Covers world literature. Includes biographies of major and minor writers, scholarly descriptions of texts written by these authors, and a variety of descriptive and critical essays on literary, cultural and historical matters to provide an understanding of the social contexts in which this writing was produced.

Additional Information

For off-campus access:

  1. From the top horizontal menu option "Subscribe," click "Individual."
  2. On that page, click "Please click here to create a membership account."
  3. On the page headed "Subscribe with us," provide the information requested. You must use a UAlberta email account.
  4. Click "Submit."
  5. Go to your UAlberta email and click the link in the validation email sent by The Literary Encyclopedia.
