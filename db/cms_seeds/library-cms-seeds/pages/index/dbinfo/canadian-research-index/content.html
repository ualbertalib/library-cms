Canadian Research Index (1982 to the present) provides access to Canadian government publications at the municipal, provincial and federal levels. It includes policy papers, scientific and technical reports, statistical reports, annual reports, etc.

Please note: The Microlog number can be used to locate the documents. If the document is not available online, submit an Interlibrary Loan Request and we will find a copy for you. Please include the Microlog ID in the patron notes.

For assistance please contact:
Humanities and Social Sciences Library Service Desk, Rutherford North: 492-5791
