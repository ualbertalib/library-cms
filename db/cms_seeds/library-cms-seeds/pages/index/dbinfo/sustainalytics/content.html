Description

Sustainalytics (formerly The Canadian Social Investment Database, CSID) contains social and environmental profiles of companies in the TSX Composite Index.

Sustainalytics can be directly accessed through its native interface. In addition, current University staff and students can download the Sustainalytics data sets:
