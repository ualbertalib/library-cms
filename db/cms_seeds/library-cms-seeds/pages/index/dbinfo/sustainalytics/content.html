Sustainalytics: Historical Datasets

Description

Sustainalytics contains social and environmental profiles of companies in the TSX Composite Index. Sustainalytics historical datasets can be downloaded by UofA faculty, students and staff:

  • 2004-2008
  • 2009-2011
  • 2012-2015

PLEASE NOTE: UofA access to the current Sustainalytics database has unfortunately been cancelled, due to an ongoing access issue with the publisher.
