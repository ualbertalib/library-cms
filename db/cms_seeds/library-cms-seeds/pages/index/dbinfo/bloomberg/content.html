University of Alberta Library Home > Databases > Bloomberg

Current and historical price data, news, indices, charts, and technical indicators on securities, including stocks (equities), bonds, currencies and others.

Location: The Bloomberg terminals are located in the Alberta School of Business basement lab. Access and Remote Access to the Bloomberg terminals is only available for students from the Alberta School of Business who need them for a reason related to course material. Students should email Krystal Belitsky and Beth Boison to gain access.
