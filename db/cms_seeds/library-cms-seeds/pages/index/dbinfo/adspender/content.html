This database provides a summary of the multi-media advertising marketplace including: TV, radio, magazines, newspapers, outdoor, and Internet. Coverage also includes 5 years of national spending trends and provides information by industry, parent company, subsidiaries, advertisers, products and brands. Data can be exported in spreadsheet, ASCII, or PDF.
