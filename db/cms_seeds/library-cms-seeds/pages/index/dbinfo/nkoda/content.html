nkoda: Digital Sheet Music {{ cms:snippet db-uofa-reg}}

nkoda requires all users to register for a personal account. Viewing and interacting with music scores requires the use of the nkoda score-reader app.

Click on Get Started and complete the one-time registration, using your @ualberta.ca email.
Click on YES to Continue as a member of the University of Alberta

To search the library and interact with scores:

  1. On a computer:
    • You can search the nkoda library using the web-based platform
    • To view and interact with individual scores, click on the thumbnail image and choose one of two different methods:
      • Open in nkoda (to open the web-based score-reader app)
      • Download nkoda (to download the desktop score-reader app)

  2. On a Windows/Android mobile device, you can search and view/interact with scores in a single app:
    • Download the nkoda app for Windows/Android, available in your app store, or at https://nkoda.com/download
    • Choose one of two different methods to log in to the app:
      • Click on My Library > Log in > Continue with Email, using the email/password used to complete your one-time registration.In the INSTITUTIONAL ACCESS window that pops up, click on YES to Continue as a member of University of Alberta
      • Click on My Library > Log in > Log in with your institution, and search for University of Alberta. You will be prompted to enter your CCID. In the INSTITUTIONAL ACCESS window that pops up, click on YES to Continue as a member of University of Alberta

  3. On an iOS device, you can search and view/interact with scores in a single app:
    • Download the nkoda sheet music reader app (NOTE: ignore the app called nkoda sheet music library), available in your app store, or at https://nkoda.com/download
    • Choose one of two different methods to log in to the app:
      • Click on My Library > Log in > Continue with Email, using the email/password used to complete your one-time registration. In the INSTITUTIONAL ACCESS window that pops up, click on YES to Continue as a member of University of Alberta
      • Click on My Library > Log in > Log in with your institution, and search for University of Alberta. You will be prompted to enter your CCID. In the INSTITUTIONAL ACCESS window that pops up, click on YES to Continue as a member of University of Alberta
