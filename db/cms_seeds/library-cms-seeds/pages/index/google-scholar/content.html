Google Scholar at the University of Alberta

Get full text articles in Google Scholar

Look for ">>" next to a result to access “GetIt @ UAlberta". Use that link to get the full text from UAlberta Library.

Configure your Google Scholar account

Link your Google account to UAlberta, so the “GetIt @ UAlberta" links will always appear – even if you don’t start your search from this page. Go to your customized settings page and click Save. If you’re logged into a Google account, it will remember that setting from now on.
