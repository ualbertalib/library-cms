Scott Hours

The Health Sciences Library is located within the Walter C Mackenzie Health Sciences Centre. Enter and exit on the second floor, where you will find the Service Desk, course materials, holds, computer workstations, study space & group study rooms. Circulating books are located on Level 1A (middle floor). Study space and journals are located on Level 1 (lower floor) of the library. There is a treadmill desk located on Level 2 and a second one located in the southeast corner of Level 1A.

Do you have questions? Ask Us for help at the service desk.
