About the Library

Augustana Library provides collections and services in support of the research and teaching of students and faculty at Augustana Campus in Camrose.

The Library is located in the Forum and boasts large windows and wonderful ravine views. It provides access to computer workstations along with a variety of student work spaces, bookable study rooms, and a treadmill desk. Augustana Library is home to the Nordic Sports Library, where U of A students and staff can borrow cross country skis, snowshoes, and ice skates. Three classrooms and the Writing Centre offices are also located in the Library.
