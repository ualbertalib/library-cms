Digital Scholarship Centre (DSC)

Located on the second floor of Cameron Library, the DSC is research centre that offers a variety of makerspace equipment and digital technology. Learn new ways to support your research with the help of our qualified media and technology experts.
More about DSC

Mediated 3D Printing

Cameron Library is one of two libraries that offer mediated 3D printing services. This service is free to University of Alberta students, faculty, and staff wanting to print a 3D object for educational purposes.
More about mediated 3D Printing

William C. Wonders map collection

The William C. Wonders map collection, located on the 4th floor of Cameron Library, holds approximately 500,000 print maps, 800,000 aerial photographs and over 5,000 atlases.

About the Library

The Cameron Library provides collections and services in support of the research and teaching of the faculties in the College of Natural and Applied Sciences: Science, Engineering, and Agricultural, Life + Environmental Sciences. Collections include the William C. Wonders Map Collection and the Canadian Circumpolar Collection. Cameron Library is also the home of the Digital Scholarship Centre.
