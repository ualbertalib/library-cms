BPSC accessibility

Make an appointment

Please Note: To request a research appointment or a group exhibition visit, please write to us at bpsc@ualberta.ca

Research appointments during the regular academic year, September-March, are booked on a first-come, first-served basis. Standard reading room policies, designed to protect rare materials, are in force. From April to August, Peel is closed, and only limited remote research services are available by email. During the summer period, non-circulating materials housed at RCRF or brought in via Interlibrary Loan can only be used in the reading room at the UofA Archives.

About the BPSC

Bruce Peel Special Collections houses a world-class collection of rare books and archival materials treating a wide range of subjects. The BPSC Website offers detailed information about rare research collections, exhibitions and Peel Workshops.
