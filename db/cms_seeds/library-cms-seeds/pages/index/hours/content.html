University of Alberta Library Hours

Due to Covid-19 most of our Library locations are closed.

Chat is open:

  • Monday to Thursday: 8:00am to 8:00pm
  • Friday: 8:00am to 6:00pm
  • Saturday and Sunday: 11:00am to 6:00pm
  • Stat Holidays: Closed

The basement and first floor of Cameron Library are open with Advanced Booking (required):

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00am to 6:00pm
  • December 10th to 22nd - Monday to Friday: 8:30am to 6:00pm
  • Saturday: 11:00am to 6:00pm
  • Sundays and Stat Holidays: Closed

Occupancy of Cameron Library is restricted to current UA students and staff only and to a maximum of 150 people. Advanced booking for all study spaces is required.

Entry through CAB West doors only with valid ONEcard.

The University Archives Reading Room is open by appointment only:

To make an appointment email archives@ualberta.ca or phone 780-248-1300.

  • Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday: 9:00am to 12:00pm & 1:00pm to 3:00pm
  • Friday, Saturday, Sunday, Monday: Closed

See our COVID-19 webpage for ongoing updates

Face masks are required at all times in all areas of Cameron Library, except when seated inside individual study rooms.

Practice physical distancing. Keep at least 2 metres (about the length of a hockey stick) between you and others.

Returns can be deposited at the Rutherford Library outside book drop (east entrance) and the Augustana Library book drops. All other book drops remain closed.
