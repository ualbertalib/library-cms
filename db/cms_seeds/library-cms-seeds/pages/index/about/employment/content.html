Job Opportunities at the Library

Academic and Support Staff

All permanent and temporary positions are posted on the University of Alberta Careers website.

Full-Time Students

To qualify as a student employee, you must be registered in 3 courses per semester. Verify your status in Bear Tracks under “my class schedule.”

To apply, please fill out the Library & Museums - Student Casual Work Application Form.

Supervisors review applications based on operational requirements, and will contact applicants directly.
