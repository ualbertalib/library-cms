Featured Collections

Henderson's Directories

City directories that contain addresses of residents and businesses, as well as city information, dating as far back as the late-1800s.

Magee Photograph Collection

Photographic negatives, stereoscope cards, and various-sized prints produced by photographers from the Magee family of Browning, Montana. Made available through a partnership with the University of Lethbridge.

Edmonton Folk Music Festival Programs

First held in August 1980, the Edmonton Folk Music Festival is now one of the premier festivals of its kind in the world. Whether you are a seasoned folk fest attendee or have yet to attend, now you can revisit the history of Edmonton's legendary festival one program at a time.

University of Alberta Board of Governors' Collection

Up until 2019, the Board of Governors at the University of Alberta recognized the generous commitment of time and service of members of the board and those who have served on external committees by selecting and digitizing books in their name which were added to the Peel's Prairie Province Collection.
