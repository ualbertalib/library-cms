The Last Best West: Glimpses of the Prairie Provinces from the Golden Age of Postcards

Visit the The Last Best West exhibit »

From 3 March to 24 June 2011, the Bruce Peel Special Collections Library at the University is hosted an exhibit of historical postcards. The exhibit included almost 100 eye-catching scenes from among the many thousands of postcards in the collection that depict the culture and history of the Canadian prairies, and was curated by Edmonton's Historian Laureate, author Ken Tingley. Click here to listen to Ken Tingley speak about the process of selecting the postcards for "The Last Best West" and the importance of the exhibit and the collection.
