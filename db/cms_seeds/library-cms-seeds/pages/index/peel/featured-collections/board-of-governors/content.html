University of Alberta Board of Governors' Collection

Between 2010 - 2019 the Board of Governors at the University of Alberta recognized the generous commitment of time and service of members of the Board and those who have served on external committees by selecting and digitizing books in their name which were then added to the Peel's Prairie Provinces collection.
