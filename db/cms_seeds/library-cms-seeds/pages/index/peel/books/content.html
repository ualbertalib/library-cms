This collection includes a wide variety of monographs, ephemera, and periodicals related to the history of the Prairie Provinces.

Highlights include printed advertisements promoting local businesses, cultural events, government programs, and elections. Many documents were created or collected by educational institutions, religious organizations, associations, and unions. You'll also find reports, booklets, pamphlets, letters, and other ephemeral print materials about a range of topics.
