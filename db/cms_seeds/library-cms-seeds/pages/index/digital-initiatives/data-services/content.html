Data Services

Data Discovery and Use

The University of Alberta Library provides access to computer-readable research data from various disciplines in the social, humanities, health, physical, and life sciences. Library staff provide assistance with the location, retrieval, and use of data files in the Library collection, as well as assistance with the acquisition of data files held elsewhere.

  • Learn more about our data collections and services

Geospatial Data

We can provide support in evaluating and using software, finding, transforming and analyzing spatial data, and mapping and visualizing results.

  • Learn more about our geospatial data services

Research Data Management

The University of Alberta Library provides tools and expertise to researchers for research data management across the data lifecycle.
