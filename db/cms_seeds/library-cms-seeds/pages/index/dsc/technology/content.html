Technology

AVAILABLE TECHNOLOGIES IN THE DSC

Digitization

Digitize a variety of print materials, photographs, slides, negatives on our large flatbed scanner. Software is available on the scanning station's high-performance iMac computer to complete basic edits on the digitized materials; more specialized software is available.

DIGITIZATION

Makerspace

Use various software to create a new project and then bring it to life through 3D printing or cutting technologies available in our DSC Makerspace.

MAKERSPACE

Media Production

Explore digital media production with various technology and supports available in the DSC. This includes audio production, photography and video.

MEDIA PRODUCTION

Software

The DSC has seven high-performance computers and a wide range of specialized software available for U of A researchers, students, and staff.

SOFTWARE

Virtual Reality + Gaming

The DSC offers access to gaming consoles and our VR studio can be used for learning, testing and experiencing VR content.

VR + GAMING
