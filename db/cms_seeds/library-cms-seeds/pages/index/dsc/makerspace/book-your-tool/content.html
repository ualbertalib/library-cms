ADDITIONAL MAKERSPACES

What is Mediated vs Unmediated 3D Printing?

A mediated 3D printing service requires you to create and then submit your 3D model to a service provider. They will load your file to their printers and complete the printing process for you.

An unmediated 3D printing service requires you to obtain working knowledge of a makerspace's 3D printers so you may successfully operate and complete the printing process of your 3D model yourself; this can include the completion of online or in-person instructional modules, quizzes, and/or practical demonstrations related to proficient printer use.
