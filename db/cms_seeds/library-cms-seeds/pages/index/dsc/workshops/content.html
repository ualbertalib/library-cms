Workshops

A primary part of the Digital Scholarship Centre's mandate is to provide instruction in tools, technologies and processes being used in scholarship today.

We offer regular workshop series that focus on different tools, media and software. All workshops are offered for free and some workshops are eligible for U of A grad student professional development credit.

Workshop schedules are planned on a term-by-term basis. If you have any questions about DSC workshops please email us at dsc.library@ualberta.ca.
