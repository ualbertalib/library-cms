Classroom Spaces

We offer collaboration, classroom and studio space to support scholarly activities, including meetings, events, research, and creativity. Please note, we reserve the right to decline booking requests that do not fit our booking policies.

BOOK YOUR CLASSROOM SPACE

BOOK THE MULTIPURPOSE ROOM: 2-20B

The Multipurpose Room is a large flexible classroom with furniture that can be rearranged easily. It includes 4 large screens that can be used to present simultaneously.

Room capacity: 24 people with tables, 40 people with chair seating only.

BOOK THE VISUALIZATION ROOM: 2-20A

The Visualization Lab features a large 7.3m x 1.4m visualization wall. The room includes cameras and microphones for video conferencing or presentations.

Room capacity: 24 people with tables, 40 people with chair seating only
