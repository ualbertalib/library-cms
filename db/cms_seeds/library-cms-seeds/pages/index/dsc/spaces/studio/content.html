Our certification process has changed, effective November 17, 2025. Full details are available on our Get Certified page.

Studio Spaces

OUR SPACES

Effective Fall 2025, new users to our Audio Recording Booth or Media Studio will need to complete a two-step certification process before booking their first session.

  • First, you must complete the online module in Canvas.
  • Second, you must complete an in-person certification check with our staff.

Our Digitization Station, Gaming and Virtual Reality studios do not require completion of a certification module.

Our studio spaces are not intended to be used as study spaces: Study rooms are available on the main and basement levels of Cameron Library and other library locations.

BOOK YOUR STUDIO SPACE

No Certification Required

BOOK THE DIGITIZATION STATION: 2-20X

An Epson 12000XL-PH large format flatbed scanner is available for digitizing print materials, photographs, slides, negatives. The scanner has a 12.2″ x 17.2″ scanning area with up to 2400 dpi optical resolution.

Workstation capacity: 2 People.

BOOK THE GAMING STUDIO: 2-20N

The gaming studio includes three video game consoles: a Nintendo Switch, a PlayStation 5 and an XBox Series S. We offer a small selection of games to try.

No food or eating in the studio. Drinks with lids are permitted.

Users can book up to 2 hours per day.

Room capacity: 5 People.

BOOK THE VIRTUAL REALITY STUDIO: 2-20E1

Our VR studio is equipped with a HTC Vive Pro set, a workstation, base station, and additional screen.

First-time users must receive a brief orientation from staff before using the studio.

No food or drink in the studio.

Do not remove equipment from the room.

Users can book up to 4 hours per day.

Room capacity: 4 People.

Certification Required

GET CERTIFIED BOOK THE AUDIO RECORDING BOOTH: 2-20Q

The recording booth features equipment to support sound capture. This soundproof room is ideal for recording voice-overs for video, podcasts and music.

  • Audio-Technica ATH-M40x Headphones.
  • Audio-Technica AT4040 Condenser Microphone.
  • Scarlett 2i4 2nd Gen Digital Audio Interface.
  • Ableton Live Recording and Mixing Software.
  • Ableton Push controller (available for use in the DSC; must be requested separately).

No food or eating in the studio. Drinks with lids are permitted.

Do not unplug equipment.

Users can book up to 2 hours per day.

Room capacity: 2 People.

GET CERTIFIED BOOK THE MEDIA STUDIO: 2-20F

Intended for the creation of video and photography projects, the space includes a backdrop, professional grade lighting, microphones, an object photography light box and a mirrorless camera for still photography and video.

No food or eating in the studio. Drinks with lids are permitted.

Do not remove equipment from the room.

Allot extra time for studio set-up/take-down.

Users can book up to 4 hours per day.

Room capacity: 4 People.
