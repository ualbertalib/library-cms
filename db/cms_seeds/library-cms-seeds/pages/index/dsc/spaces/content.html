THE DSC

The DSC is an academic centre designed to facilitate scholarly activities, including research and teaching. We are governed by the U of A Library's Community Expectations.

We offer collaboration, classroom and studio space to support meetings, events, research, and creativity.

Space Booking Policies

  • Access to our collaboration rooms is limited to U of A faculty, graduate students, staff, and researchers only.
  • Requests for use of space by U of A Alumni, visiting scholars, community partners, Edmonton-based educational institutions, and members of the public will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • We reserve the right to decline booking requests that do not fit our booking policies.
    • General study room bookings for current U of A students, staff, and faculty that do not meet our guidelines are available at other U of A Library locations with your CCID.

We Can Accommodate

We Cannot Accommodate
Research, research group meetings, and research team events, such as launch celebrations, lectures, and showcases. Recurring classroom lectures or seminars; all classroom space for U of A course instruction can be booked through the Office of the Registrar.
Workshops related to technology use. Silent individual or group study.
Group work and group projects for graduate students. Administrative, committee, or governance meetings.
Recording videos, podcasts, or presentations. Recurring meetings related to the curriculum, staff development, or faculty retreats.
Testing the space and technology before a planned event. Students Union group or club meetings.
Consultations with librarians, researchers, staff, and other U of A community members. Conference organizing service requests.
Graduate student group meetings, events, and activities. Requests for events that fall outside of regular operating hours.
