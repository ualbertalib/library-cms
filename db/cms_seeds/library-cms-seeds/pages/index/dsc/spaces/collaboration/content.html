Collaboration Spaces

  • Access to our collaboration rooms is limited to U of A faculty, graduate students, staff, and researchers only.
  • Requests for use of space by U of A Alumni, visiting scholars, community partners, Edmonton-based educational institutions, and members of the public will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • We reserve the right to decline booking requests that do not fit our booking policies.
    • General study room bookings for current U of A students, staff, and faculty that do not meet our guidelines are available at other U of A Library locations with your CCID.

BOOK YOUR COLLABORATION SPACES

  • U of A faculty, graduate students, staff, and researchers may request access by emailing dsc.library@ualberta.ca.
