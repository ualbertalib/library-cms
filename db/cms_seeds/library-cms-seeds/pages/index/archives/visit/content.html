The University of Alberta Archives is open to all researchers including staff, students, and members of the public. Please see the University of Alberta's HealthyU Essentials for information about health and safety requirements.

Reading Room Hours


Tuesday – Thursday

9:00 AM – 3:00 PM (closed over the lunch hour from 12–1)

by appointment only


Due to the secure nature of the facility, drop-in access is not permitted.

To make an appointment to view material, please contact us at archives@ualberta.ca or by phone at 780-248-1300. Please allow 48 hours for staff to pull material.
