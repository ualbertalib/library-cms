Article Processing Charge Support

Many journals require article processing charges (APCs) for Open Access. Paying APCs is one way for authors to have their publications released openly (ie. freely available online, with no paywall).

Wherever possible, the University of Alberta Library negotiates with publishers so that U of A corresponding authors receive APC-free publishing, or APC discounts, as part of the Library’s ongoing agreements with these publishers.

Introducing a new tool for University of Alberta Researchers:

Search APC-Free or APC-Discounted Journals

This page lists the publishers who currently provide APC-free or APC-discounted publishing for U of A corresponding authors.

Please note: The waivers and discounts referred to here apply only to article processing charges for Open Access publishing. Any additional publisher charges (e.g., colour charges) must be paid by the author. The Library makes every effort to provide up-to-date information, and cannot be held responsible for any APC charges resulting from publisher changes to the status of individual journals.

This page has recently been updated and revised. If you have feedback, please let us know via our Ask Us or see our FAQs re: APCs.

APC-Free or APC-Discounted Publishing

1. American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS)
  • U of A corresponding authors receive a 15% APC discount with Science Advances.
  • An additional 4% APC discount is available for AAAS members.
2. American Chemical Society (ACS)
  • U of A corresponding authors receive an APC discount of $250 USD.
  • An additional $250 USD APC discount is available for personal ACS members.
  • Search the APC-Free or APC-Discounted Journal Search Tool for titles.
3. Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)
  • APC-free publishing with all ACM publications, including journals, magazines, and conference or workshop proceedings.
  • Search the APC-Free or APC-Discounted Journal Search Tool for titles.
4. Biochemical Society Journals (Portland Press)
  • APC-free publishing available with five (5) journals.
  • Search the APC-Free or APC-Discounted Journal Search Tool for titles.
5. Canadian Science Publishing
  • APC-free publishing available with five (5) journals.
  • Most other journals provide a 25% APC discount.
  • Search the APC-Free or APC-Discounted Journal Search Tool for titles.
