University of Alberta Library Home > Library Services > Open Access

Open access is the practice of providing free and unrestricted online access to research publications and data.

You will find OA publications in these formats:

  • Journals make articles immediately available online
  • Authors self-archive articles in a subject-based repository (e.g. arXiv.org)
  • Authors self-archive articles in an institutional repository (e.g. ERA)
