Research Support

The Library supports activities throughout the research and scholarly communication lifecycle:

Explore, Plan, and Organize

  • In-depth research consultations with subject librarians, including support for literature searching
  • Extensive collections and databases supporting thorough and up-to-date literature searches
  • Citation and reference management assistance
  • Consultations and guidance on research data management and data management planning

Conducting and Analyzing

  • Workshops covering essential research skills
  • Consultations and guidance about digital research technologies available in the Digital Scholarship Centre

Distribution and Publishing

  • Supports for publishing Open Access (OA), including APC-free and APC-discounted publishing in over ten thousand journals
  • OA publishing program home to over seventy journals, Open Educational Resources (OER) including open textbooks
  • University of Alberta Press
  • Copyright support for researchers

Impact and Engagement

  • Research Impact and Digital Scholarship Centre services help communicate the impact of your research
  • Support for ORCID (researcher and contributor standardized identification)

Preservation and Stewardship

  • Infrastructure and services that help ensure stable, long-term analog and digital preservation, including Borealis-Dataverse (data repository), ERA (institutional repository), and Aviary (audio and video curation)
