Online Download Service

When possible, we provide downloads of requested articles and book chapters from the collections from other libraries. Access the requested item without coming to the Library. Please note the instructions in your email, as the links will expire!

UAlberta library materials scanning service for UAlberta students and staff:

You may request a scan of a book chapter or print journal article that is held in the UAlberta Library collection by completing the interlibrary loan form linked above.
