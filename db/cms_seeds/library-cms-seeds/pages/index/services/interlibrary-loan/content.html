Online Download Service

When possible, we provide downloads of requested articles and book chapters from the collections from other libraries. Access the requested item without coming to the Library. Please note the instructions in your email, as the links will expire!

University of Alberta library materials scanning service for U of A students and staff:

You may request a scan of a book chapter or print journal article that is held in the U of A Library collection by completing the interlibrary loan form linked above.

Content will be reproduced and provided or communicated pursuant to the University of Alberta Fair Dealing Guidelines. Questions? Contact ill@ualberta.ca.
