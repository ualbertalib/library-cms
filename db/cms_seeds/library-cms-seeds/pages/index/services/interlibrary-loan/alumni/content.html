Interlibrary Loan for Alumni

A limited Interlibrary Loan service is available to University of Alberta Alumni at no cost through the Alumni library account. The service is limited in that we will only search libraries in North America.

To access this service, you will need a current Alumni library account. If you do not have one, please visit the ONEcard office (INFOlink in HUBmall) to obtain an ALUMNIcard, or visit a library service desk to learn about your options. Please note that the Alumni eResource service is not an Alumni library account.

Submit interlibrary loan requests using the "Login with Barcode and PIN" button on the ILL homepage. Use your barcode and PIN from your Alumni library account to log into the service. If you're not sure what your barode or PIN is, please ask for assistance.
