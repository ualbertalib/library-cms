Interlibrary Loan for Alumni

A limited Interlibrary Loan service* is available to University of Alberta Alumni at no cost.

To access this service, you will need a current Alumni library account. If you do not have one, please fill out this form to inquire about your options: Account Inquiry / Fines Appeal

Submit ILL requests via the "Login with Barcode and PIN" button on our ILL homepage. Use your Barcode and PIN from your Alumni ONEcard library account OR please ask for assistance .

*Searches for Alumni are limited to North American locations.
