Home Delivery Eligibility & Registration

Have print materials delivered to a home or work address, for free, if you are a: University of Alberta Student, and

  • reside 50km or more from Edmonton, and
  • have a valid U of A Library ID (the 13-digit barcode number on your ONEcard).

U of A Faculty or Staff member, and

  • involved in delivering distance courses or programs,
  • need to be at course sites 50km or more from Edmonton, and
  • have a valid U of A Library ID (the 13-digit barcode number on your ONEcard).
