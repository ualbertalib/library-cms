Address/Status Changes

When your address changes from the one you registered with as a distance patron, it needs to be updated...

  • Change your address in Bear Tracks
  • Contact Distance Services at depository@ualberta.ca

Contact us at depository@ualberta.ca if you no longer want to be registered as a distance patron.
