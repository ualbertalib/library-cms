AUGUSTANA LIBRARY
https://library.ualberta.ca/locations/augustana

DESIGNATED ACCESSIBLE PARKING: Visitor parking is available in the Visitor Parking area in the K. Glen Johnson Faith and Life Parking Lot. There are four designated wheelchair parking spots near the entrance to the Faith and Life Centre. Indoor access to the library from Visitor Parking is through the Faith and Life Centre into the Forum where the library is .

RAMP ACCESS TO BUILDING OR LIBRARY: At the door entrance to Faith and Life Centre are automatic push buttons that users need to press. In addition to the Faith and Life Centre entrance, there is wheelchair access from outside on the south side of the Forum building. The double set of doors have an automatic push plate that users need to press. There is a wheelchair ramp leading from the doors to near the library entrance. The first part of the ramp has large mats as a non-slip surface.

ELEVATORS: There is an elevator at the rear left of the library. It is wheelchair accessible. The elevator accesses the lower basement level (the main stacks and study areas) and the second floor Learning Commons and Classroom area.

LOW HEIGHT SERVICE DESK: The service desk is at the library's main entrance on the main floor. There are three stations there, and one of them is a wheelchair height service desk.

ACCESSIBLE WASHROOMS: There are women's and men's washrooms on the main floor of the library (behind the glass wall to the right when entering the library), and on the basement level (at the back left near the laptop bar). The washrooms are wheelchair accessible with privacy entrance (no physical door). They all have push button soap dispensors and automatic hand dryers. The main floor washroom additionally has a paper hand towel dispensor.

ADJUSTABLE HEIGHT WORK TABLES: Two adjustable height work tables on the main floor (can be used with laptops) at the back of the library along east wall facing the ravine (near the elevator). They are both table style; one has a manual lever to adjust height and the other is electric push button.


OVERSIZED, WALL-MOUNTED MONITORS: Two bookable group rooms with 55 inch Kramer screens with wireless connectivity (CCID login needed). One room (Presentation Practice Room) is on the library's main floor in the far right corner. The other room (Group Room F) is on the lower basement floor along east wall facing the ravine. Capacity of each room is 12 people and can be booked for 2 hours.

LOW HEIGHT WATER FOUNTAINS OR WATER BOTTLE DISPENSERS: Two water fountains at wheelchair height beside washrooms on main floor. There are push buttons to operate. Each fountain has an integrated water dispenser for water bottles.

AREAS INACCESSIBLE BY WHEELCHAIR: The 24 hour study space does not have automatic doors and does not have easy door opening access when the library is closed.
