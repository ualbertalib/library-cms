Multimedia Lending at the Library

Multimedia equipment can be borrowed at library service desks by University of Alberta students, staff, and faculty only. They cannot be placed on hold. Items have a 72 hour loan period unless otherwise noted. For more information on late fees visit our Borrowing Privileges Guide.

If you have any questions about the technology and equipment available at our libraries don’t hesitate to Ask Us.


Items available to borrow include:

  • Cameras & Tripods
  • Audio Recording Equipment
  • Robotics Kits
  • Media viewing equipment, calculators, and more
