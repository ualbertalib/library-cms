Microfilm - Microfiche - Microcard

UARCRF Micro Requests (Microform/Newspapers)

  • Newspaper items need additional publication information (e.g., year, specific date, section, article title, etc.) for us to retrieve the item you need.
  • Please use the RCRF Microform/Newspaper Request Form
  • Service Standard: Available for pickup within two to three work days (Monday through Friday) at your selected pickup location.

Borrowing Privileges

  • The robotics and related kits may be borrowed by University of Alberta students, staff, and faculty only.
  • The kits must be returned to the owning library's service desk.
  • Loan period is 1 week.
  • $1/hour per overdue item.
  • No renewals.
  • No holds.
  • Damaged items will be assessed to determine what fees will apply.
  • Replacement charges will be applied in place of the $150 lost charge that is indicated in your library account.
  • Borrowers are subject to the University of Alberta Libraries borrowing policies.

The Library no longer lends laptops. The Dean of Students has a limited number of chromebooks & laptops available for students in need.

Fax Machines

The U of A Library does not offer fax services. Public fax machines are located at:

  • Varsity Health/Stationary in HUB Mall (#39, just across from Rutherford Library). Open evenings and weekends.
  • SUBprint in the Student's Union Building basement. Open Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm.
  • Canada Post Office, Shopper's Drug Mart located at 8210 109 street (off-campus). Open evenings and weekends.

SMART Boards

SMART Boards are available for use in the following library: Bibliothèque Saint-Jean. To reserve a study space with a SMART Board, book the appropriate room using the online reservation system.

Bibliothèque Saint-Jean

1-12E: capacity of up to 12 people. SMART Board pens, keyboard and mouse are available at the service desk.
