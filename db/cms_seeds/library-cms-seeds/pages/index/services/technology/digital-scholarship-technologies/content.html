Digital Scholarship Technologies

3D Printing

The University of Alberta Library has different 3D printing service points. All 3D printing locations offer both PLA filament and resin printing. Students, staff, and faculty from the University of Alberta community can access our 3D printing services for free.

Staff print users' designs in mediated locations, while users learn to print themselves in unmediated locations. To certify for unmediated 3D printing, please enroll in the desired module through the DSC website.

If you are interested in makerspace environments, visit these other University locations:

  • Augustana Campus 3D Printshop
  • Elko Engineering Garage
  • The Shack (Faculty of Science)

Media Spaces

Whether you're interested in podcasting, digital storytelling, or exploring virtual reality environments, the University of Alberta Library has specific spaces designed to let you try your hand at digital media technology. Please note that spaces must be booked ahead of time using the library booking system.

Don't know where to start? The DSC offers research communication workshops on different media tools, such as video making.
