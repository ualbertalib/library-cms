Specialized Equipment

There are a number of different technologies you can interact with in our spaces beyond Public Computing Stations & Printers including:

  • Listening and Viewing Equipment
  • Microform Readers and Scanners
Equipment at the library is available on a first come, first serve basis unless otherwise noted. Library staff are more than happy to provide assistance.

If you have any questions about the technology and equipment available at our libraries don’t hesitate to Ask Us.

Microfilm/Microfiche/Microcard Access

Much of our microformat collection is in storage at the RCRF. Please use the RCRF Microform/Newspaper Request Form to request microformat materials be sent to the campus libray of your choice. Requests typically take 2 to 3 business days to be available for pickup.Requests for newspaper items require additional publication information (ex. year, specific date, section, article, title) for staff to retrieve the correct item.

Both Augustana and Rutherford libraries have devices for viewing microformat materials.

Augustana Library

Scanpro 3000 for fiche & microfilm

  • Save as a PDF, multi-page PDF, and more.
Rutherford Library

ViewScan4 (2 stations), fiche, microfilm, microcards

  • ViewScan4 Quick Start Guide
