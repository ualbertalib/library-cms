Printing, Scanning, and Photocopying

Printing, copying, and scanning services are available at ONEcard printers located across campus.

For detailed instructions visit the ONEcard website.

Costs for ONEcard Printing and Photocopying

Copying fees will be debited from your ONEcard Cash account.

$0.12 per side for B&W.

$0.35 per side for colour.

For 3D printing visit the 3D Printing website.

For Large Format Printing (and more), visit SUBprint.

Flatbed Scanning

Flatbed scanners locations:

  • Augustana Library
  • Cameron Library Knowledge Common
  • Law Library
  • Rutherford South Lab 2-03 and 2-05

Detailed instructions can be found next to each scanner workstation, and staff at the service desks are happy to assist with any questions.
