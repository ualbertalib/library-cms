Continuing + Professional Education Students

  • Continuing & Professional Education students enrolled in a diploma program may obtain a ONEcard from the ONEcard Office . These students have the same borrowing privileges as U of A students
  • Continuing & Professional Education students in all other courses may get a Public Borrower or an Alberta Library Card

Access to Online Resources

Remote access to library licensed online resources is available to some Continuing & Professional Education students based on your program. Please enquire at the Continuing & Faculty of Extension for details.

All Continuing & Professional Education students may access library licensed online resources such as databases or eBooks on-site, from a library computer terminal or using the wireless network on your laptop (UWS). To access these resources, please visit a library service desk to obtain a temporary (daily) Library Network Access ID. You will be asked to present your photo ID (eg. driver's license) or your U of A library card.
