Borrower Services + Library Cards

The University of Alberta Library welcomes the Students, Faculty, and Staff of the University of Alberta to use their ONECard and/or their CCID to access our world class collection.

  • U of A Library Cards + Access
  • U of A Loan Periods
  • U of A Recalls
  • U of A Fines

We also provide some library access to:

  • U of A Alumni
  • The Alberta Library
  • NEOS Member Libraries
  • Members of the Public
  • Continuing + Professional Education Students
  • University of Calgary Social Work Students

In addition, the Library participates in a number of Reciprocal Agreements with other university libraries.

If you have any questions about Library Cards, Library Access, or Borrower Terms, please don't hesitate to Ask Us or use our Live Chat during chat hours to speak with a library staff member.
