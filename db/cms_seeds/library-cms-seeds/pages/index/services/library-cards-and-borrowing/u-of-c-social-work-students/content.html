U of C Social Work Students

  • University of Calgary Social Work BSW students and Faculty/Instructors, please see the University of Alberta Students, Academics & Staff page for information borrowing privileges
  • University of Calgary MSW students, please see the Members of the Public page for information about library cards and borrowing privileges.

Use your ONEcard to borrow library materials

University of Calgary BSW students receive a ONEcard. If your card has not been registered, please fill out this form: Account Inquiries

Access to University of Alberta Library Online Resources

University of Calgary Social Work students may access library licensed online resources on-site only. To access these resources, visit a library service desk to obtain a temporary (daily) Library Network Access ID. You will be asked to present your photo ID (eg. driver's license).
