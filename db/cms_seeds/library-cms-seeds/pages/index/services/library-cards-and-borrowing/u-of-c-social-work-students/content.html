Borrowing Privileges - U of C Social Work Students

  • U of Calgary Social Work BSW and Faculty/Instructors, please see the UAlberta Students, Academics & Staff page for information borrowing privileges.
  • U of Calgary MSW students, please see the Members of the Public page for information about library cards and borrowing privileges.

Use your ONEcard to borrow library materials

U of Calgary BSW students receive a ONEcard which we manually register in the Library user database before the beginning of term. If you find that your card has not been registered, please contact the Social Work Librarian.
