Patrons of NEOS Member Libraries

View loan periods information

If you are visiting a University of Alberta Library from a NEOS Member Institution: Please visit the service desk the first time you want to borrow items to verify whether you have a registered account, or if account registration is required. Please provide your NEOS Institution card and proof of address for account registration. We are happy to serve you.

NEOS Member Libraries borrowers are not eligible to borrow University of Alberta course materials or multimedia technology.
