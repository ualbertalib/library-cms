Overdue + Lost Items

U of A Libraries does not charge fines for overdue items. However you will be charged for lost items.

At 31 days overdue, you will receive a lost item notice ($150/item - higher charges may be assessed based on cost). This blocks your library and university account. Note: Overdue course materials, equipment and robotics will block your account on the same day it is due.

Once you return your item or pay your fee, the library block is removed immediately and the University block is removed when the system updates every 6 hours (block lifted at 8 am, 2 pm, 8 pm, and 2 am).

Please fill out our Accounts Inquiries form if you have questions about lost items or blocks.

Term Loan Due Dates

(For U of A staff and students)

Fall Term: January 31
Winter Term: May 31
Spring + Summer Terms: September 29

Recalled items are due within one week of receiving notice. Due dates for all other borrowers vary based on checkout date.
