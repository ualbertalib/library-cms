Human Resources for Managers + Supervisors

UofA Resources for Managing Employees can be found at Human Resources, Health, Safety + Environment - Resources for Managing Employees

Hiring/Recruitment

  • If you are a new supervisor or manager please contact libprsnl@ualberta.ca to ensure your access is correct, resource materials are shared and you are added to appropriate drives, groups etc.
  • Casual Student Hiring
    • Complete Student Hiring Request Form for student casual hires that are pre-approved by your AUL/Budget holder.
      • Please Remember: This form is for all STUDENT casual hires, and extensions ONLY. For all other hiring actions including non-student casual hires please email libprsnl@ualberta.ca
    • Remember to ensure the hiring process is completed in a timely manner in terms of the employees start date and pay. Please refer to the payroll and APS deadlines in the link: Payroll and APS deadlines
  • Temporary, Casual or Permanent/Continuing Position (Non-Student)
    • Email libprsnl@ualberta.ca when you are approved to hire a non-student casual or permanent staff to initiate the process.
  • Please review UofA Hiring Website for more details that will be required for your recruitment.

Managing Vacations, Leaves + Voluntary PLP

  • Information about leaves and vacation entitlement varies by employment type. See University of Alberta benefits and pay info at: Human Resources, Health, Safety and Environment - Benefits + Pay
  • Should an incident take place where an employee has a work related injury or illness, the staff member must notify their supervisor and LibHR libprsnl@ualberta.ca must be contacted as soon as possible.
  • More information regarding illness and injury leave can be found at: Human Resources, Health, Safety and Environment - Illness + Injury Leave
