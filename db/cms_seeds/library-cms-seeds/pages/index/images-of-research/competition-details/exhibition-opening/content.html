Exhibition Opening

YOU ARE INVITED

Please join us in celebrating the 2025 Images of Research competition at our Exhibition Opening!

RSVPs are now closed

The Exhibition Opening will take place on Thursday, May 29th at 2:00PM MST in the University of Alberta Library's Digital Scholarship Centre (DSC) on the 2nd Floor of Cameron Library.

The 24 semi-finalist entries will be on display throughout the DSC's space, and the Committee will announce this year's first, second, third, and People's Choice winners.

The Exhibition

The 2025 semi-finalists and winners will remain on display in the DSC during their hours of operation until end-of-day on Friday, June 13th.
