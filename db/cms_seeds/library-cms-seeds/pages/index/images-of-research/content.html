About Images of Research

Images of Research (IOR) is an opportunity for current University of Alberta (U of A) graduate students from all disciplines to capture, share and present the essence of their research in one image.

The IOR competition, digital exhibition and showcase for semi finalists preserve graduate research in digital form, providing an opportunity for graduate students to communicate their research in a different medium and capture the attention of new audiences.

A multidisciplinary jury will select the semi finalists and winners according to the following criteria:

  • Overall originality/creativity of the submission
  • Aesthetic appeal of the image
  • Relationship between image and student’s research
  • Clarity of title and written description

Four prizes will be awarded as follows:

  • First Place - $800
  • Second Place - $600
  • Third Place - $500
  • (1) People’s Choice Award (determined by an online public vote) - $250

Competition 2024 Timelines (all times based on Edmonton time, MST) :

  • Tuesday, April 2, 2024 at 9 a.m. - Submissions open
  • REVISED DATE: Sunday, April 28, 2024 at 5 p.m. - Submissions close
  • Monday, May 13, 2024 at 9 a.m. to Sunday, May 26, 2024 at 5 p.m. - People's Choice Voting
  • Thursday, May 30, 2024 at 2 p.m. - Celebration Event at the U of A Library's Digital Scholarship Centre

The Images of Research competition is organized by the University of Alberta Library (UAL) and the Faculty of Graduate & Postdoctoral Studies (GPS). Past submissions can be viewed on the University of Alberta Library's website.

For any questions, please contact Jay Friesen, PD Instructional Designer (GPS) at jayf@ualberta.ca.
