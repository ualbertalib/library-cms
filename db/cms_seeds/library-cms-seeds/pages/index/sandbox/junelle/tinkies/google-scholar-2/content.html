Google Scholar

CONFIGURE YOUR ACCOUNT

Get Full Text Articles in Google Scholar

  • Link your Google account* to UAlberta, so the “GetIt @ UAlberta" links will always appear – even if you don’t start your search from this page.
  • Go to your customized settings page and click Save. If you’re logged into a Google account, it will remember that setting from now on.

    *YOU MUST USE YOUR UALBERTA GOOGLE ACCOUNT; NOT YOUR PERSONAL GMAIL.

SEARCH GOOGLE SCHOLAR

AFTER CONFIGURATION

  • Enter your keywords into the Google Scholar search bar (article title, search terms, author name) and click search
  • Look for ">>" next to a result
  • Select ">>". If “GetIt @ UAlberta" appears, select that link to get the full text from U of A Library.
