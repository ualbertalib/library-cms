Google Scholar at the U of A

Configure Your Account To Get Full Text Articles in Google Scholar

  • Link your Google account to UAlberta, so the “GetIt @ UAlberta" links will always appear – even if you don’t start your search from this page.
  • Go to your customized settings page and click Save. If you’re logged into a Google account, it will remember that setting from now on.

To Search Google Scholar

  • Look for ">>" next to a result to access “GetIt @ UAlberta". Use that link to get the full text from UAlberta Library.
