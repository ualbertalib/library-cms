Configure Your U of A Account

Accessing full text articles via Google Scholar is easier when you configure your U of A account.

  • Link your Google account to UAlberta, so the “GetIt @ UAlberta" links will always appear – even if you don’t start your search from this page.
  • Go to your customized settings page and click Save. If you’re logged into your U of A Google account, it will remember that setting from now on.
