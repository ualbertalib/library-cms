Digital Scholarship Centre

The Digital Scholarship Centre (DSC) is a research and teaching support service for students and faculty at the University of Alberta. The DSC offers physical and digital spaces for the university community across all disciplines to create, collaborate, and explore new ideas.

Please visit the Digital Scholarship Centre Website for more information or to book spaces and equiptment.
