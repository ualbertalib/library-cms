Hours & Locations
Library Reopening Plan

The UAlberta Library is excited to announce that we will begin reopening locations to in-person visits starting August 16th.
See our Reopening Plan for details and upcoming hours.

Cameron

Sciences, Engineering, & Business Library

  • Cameron Library, first floor and basement, are open for individual study. Access is by ONEcard prox/swipe and advance bookings are required. Effective July 26, staff and students will no longer be required to book a study space in Cameron Library. Most spaces will be first-come, first-serve. Study rooms will still be available for booking.
  • Monday to Friday: 10:00am to 5:00pm
  • Saturday, Sunday and Stat Holidays: Closed
  • Monday, August 2 - Closed for Stat Holiday (Heritage Day)
  • Entry only available via the Cameron southwest exterior doors; then swipe/tap with valid ONEcard at the internal door.
    Note: There is no access to Cameron from CAB.
  • Effective July 1, 2021: While masks are no longer required, we strongly encourage wearing of face masks while in Cameron Library.
  • Practice physical distancing. Keep at least 2 metres (about the length of a hockey stick) between you and others.
