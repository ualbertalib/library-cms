eResources for Alumni
Alumni Borrowing Services | UAlberta Alumni

Access to tens of thousands of full text resources: scholarly journals, books, primary source collections, and more. These resources cover a wide range of disciplines and are available exclusively to UAlberta Alumni. Continue your path of lifelong learning from anywhere in the world, 24 hours a day, seven days a week, and without late fees!
