COVID-19 Library Service Updates

-- Last updated: March 20, 2020 --

Online Collection and Services are still available. Services will be evolving as the situation develops. We will keep this page up-to-date as additional information becomes available.

Use the quick links below for more information.

Library Collections | Library Hours & Spaces | Due Dates & Late Fees

Library Collections

  • Online collections are available from the Library Homepage.
  • Guides to Library Resources By Subject
  • All physical Library collections (print books, music, etc) are unavailable. No holds or returns (please see Due Dates and Late Fees below)
  • Access to Course Materials
  • Inter-Library Loan requests will continue with delivery in digital format to the extent possible. Please continue to submit your requests.
  • Archives and Bruce Peel Special Collections are closed.
  • Equipment lending is closed to help mitigate infection spread risks.
  • Picking up 3d printed models is not available at this time, and submitting new jobs is discouraged.

Library Hours and Spaces

All of our library locations are closed indefinitely. Cameron Library main floor and basement are available for students who do not have computer access at home.

Cameron Library hours:

  • ONEcard swipe access only for UAlberta students, faculty, and staff, main floor and basement. See hours here.
  • Computers available on main floor and basement

Cameron Library cleaning:

  • Computer keyboards, mice and screens will be disinfected after each student use.
  • A Bee-Clean staff member will be on duty to provide cleaning most hours when the library is open. Special attention will be paid to all high-touch surfaces such as door handles, table surfaces, bathrooms, and cell-phone charging lockers.

Due dates and late fees

  • All items you have signed out now have a due date of May 31, 2020.
  • All Library Drop Boxes (Return Slots) are closed. Please do not return items.
  • Late fees:
    • The library will cancel late fees from March 13 until the situation changes. Again, you do not need to come to campus.
    • For previously incurred fines, UA Staff and Students can pay them here.
    • For more help with fines, please Ask Us or fill out this form to explain your situation
    • We realize that library fines that have accumulated since March 13 may impede your ability to register for Fall/Winter classes. We are working on a solution. We will work with you to remove any financial holds that the library has placed on your account as a result of this situation. For help, please contact us.
