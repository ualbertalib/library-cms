Study Room Rules

  • Study rooms are available for booking during library hours.
  • Study rooms may be booked up to seven days in advance; no student or group may make more than one booking per day. Block booking of rooms (i.e. one booking per each student group member) is not acceptable.
  • Please cancel your booking if you no longer need to use the space. A 15-minute grace period will be given for all room bookings and occupancy, after which time your booking is considered forfeited to others. Library staff may delete the booking so that another may book the room.
  • To ensure equitable access to study rooms, especially during exam times, guidelines may be revised.
  • Rooms are not secure; U of A Library is not responsible for loss or damage of items left unattended in the room.
  • Please remove all personal items and garbage when vacating rooms. Please take care of the equipment and facilities so that we can continue to offer you these services.
