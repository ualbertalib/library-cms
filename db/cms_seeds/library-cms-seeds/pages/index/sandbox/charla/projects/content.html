Archive-It is a service that helps to harvest, build, and preserve collections of digital content.

AtoM facilitates searching archival records and collections at the University of Alberta.

Provides long-term preservation of the research data from CWRC.

Integrating library services into the U of A's Learning Management System.

Linked Data Implementation Plan

The Linked Data Implementation Plan describes the steps the UAlberta Library Cataloguing & Metadata Strategies unit is taking to achieve the outcomes and goals outlined in Moving Forward with Linked Data at the University of Alberta Library.
