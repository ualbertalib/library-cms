Cameron (Science & Technology)

  • Automatic door at main entrance on the southwest corner of the Cameron Library building, with external and internal ramps - accessible lift located inside this entrance provides access to main and lower levels (see note) - additional ramp entrance via the pedway from the Central Academic Building (CAB)
  • Accessible men's and women's washrooms on the lower floor, adjacent to the accessible lift at the front door.
  • Wheelchair accessible water fountain between these washrooms.
  • one height adjustable table on level one in the Fireside lounge and two more on level three.
  • One accessible Kiosk inside the main library entrance.

Rutherford (Social Science & Humanities)

  • From HUB Mall - take elevator at entrance 9002 to ground level. Rutherford Library east entrance is directly across the path.
  • Wheelchair accessible water fountain between these washrooms.
  • one height adjustable table on level one in the Fireside lounge and two more on level three.
  • One accessible Kiosk inside the main library entrance.

Scott (Health Sciences)

  • Automatic door (push plate) at the entrance & exit to the library.
  • The elevator is at the end of the corridor beside the Service Desk and stops on all 3 levels of the library.
  • There is a wheelchair accessible service desk on the second level (entrance level).
  • Wheelchair accessible water fountain between these washrooms.
  • one height adjustable table on level one in the Fireside lounge and two more on level three.
  • One accessible Kiosk inside the main library entrance.
