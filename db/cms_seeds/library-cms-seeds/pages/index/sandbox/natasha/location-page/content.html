The Sperber Health Sciences Library offers modern library services and collections, features an exhibit display of the Dentistry Museum Collection, and accommodates various learning and collaboration needs.

The entrance to the Health Sciences Library is on the ground level of the Edmonton Clinic Health Academy (ECHA) building directly across from the Starbucks.
