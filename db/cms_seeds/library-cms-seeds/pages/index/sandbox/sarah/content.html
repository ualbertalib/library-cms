Textbook affordability ....


For students

  • Zero Textbook Cost (ZTC) Label in Bear Tracks - Choose your classes based on course material costs! Any class marked ZTC does not have required reading materials that you need to buy.

For instructors

  • Find or Publish Open Educational Resources - Make your class ZTC! Explore resources you can adopt for your course or learn more about the Library's open textbook publishing program.
  • Create a Reading List - Bring all your free course materials together in one place.
  • Reserve Course Materials - Make sure your textbook is available on short term loan through the library for your class to access it without having to purchase it.
