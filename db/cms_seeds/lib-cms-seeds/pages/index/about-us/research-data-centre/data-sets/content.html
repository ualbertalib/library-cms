Available Data

The University of Alberta Research Data Centre offers researchers secure access to detailed micro-data from population and household surveys, censuses and administrative files. A detailed listing of Statistics Canada surveys available for use at the Research Data Centre can be found at at the Statistics Canada RDC data sets page . For information about the content of the data there are various presentations.

The Canadian Research Data Center Network (CRDCN) has a catagorised listing of the data available in the RDC including census and administrative data.

Data Security

The University of Alberta Research Data Centre operate in accordance with the confidentiality provisions of the Statistics Act and is accessible only to researchers with approved projects and security clearance. The data can only be accessed within the Research Data Centre and results must first be vetted by the Analyst before they can be removed.
