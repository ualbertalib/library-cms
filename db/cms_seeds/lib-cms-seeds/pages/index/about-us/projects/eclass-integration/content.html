eClass Integration - Library Resources Block

Custom resources for your course

The library resource block in your eClass course can be customized to add links to course-specific resources (e.g. links to subject resource guides, databases, tutorials, or subject librarian introduction videos). Students can then access these resources from their eClass course.

Enrol your subject librarian as a designer

Once enrolled as a "Designer" your subject librarian can then add links to relevant course resources as in the example below.

  • Identify your subject librarian
  • Enrol them as a Designer in your eClass course

NOTE: If you copied content from an existing course that did not have a library resources block, you can add it yourself using the instructions below or your subject librarian can add the block for you once they are enrolled as a Designer.
