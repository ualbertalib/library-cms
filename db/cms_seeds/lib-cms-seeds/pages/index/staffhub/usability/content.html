Discovery Usability Study 2017/18

Take a look at our Discovery Usability Study Report. The report is based on hands-on user testing consisting of 30 formal lab-based usability tests with Discovery end users (undergraduate students, graduate students, and faculty) of up to 1 hour in length. The report includes a series of recommendations for changes to our discovery system to address issues that were identified from an in-depth analysis of the results of the testing.
