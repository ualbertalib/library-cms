Canadian Research Index (1982 to the present) provides access to Canadian government publications at the municipal, provincial and federal levels. It includes policy papers, scientific and technical reports, statistical reports, annual reports, etc.

Please note: The Microlog number can be used to locate the documents, which are available in the Microlog microfiche collection. The Microlog microfiche collection is located on the 2nd floor of Rutherford Library North.
