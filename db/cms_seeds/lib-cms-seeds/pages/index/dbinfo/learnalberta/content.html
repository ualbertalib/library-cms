LearnAlberta.ca provides high quality digital learning and teaching resources uniquely correlated to Alberta Education's Kindergarten to Grade 12 programs of study.

Conditions of Use:
Use of this product is restricted to students, faculty, and staff of the University of Alberta and requires login with a current CCID. It is the responsibility of each user to ensure that they use this product for individual, non-commercial educational or research purposes only, and do not systematically download or retain substantial portions of information.

Please Note:
An access ID and password is required to use LearnAlberta.ca and can only be issued to faculty, instructors, and students teaching or enrolled in at least one course that is either offered by the Faculty of Education or eligible for credit towards a B.Ed degree. Individual access IDs and passwords expire every 6 months, and when they expire you will have to contact the H.T. Coutts Library for a new ID/password combination.

To obtain your access ID and password, please contact the H. T. Coutts (Education & Physical Education) library Information Desk. Upon presentation of your ONEcard and determination of your eligibility, you will be given the ID and password. Eligible users may also obtain their ID and password by calling 492-3770 or via email at educref@ualberta.ca.

When e-mailing your request, please include your Name, Phone Number, ONECard Number, e-mail address, and status (Faculty, Undergrad, or Graduate Student, and your department).
