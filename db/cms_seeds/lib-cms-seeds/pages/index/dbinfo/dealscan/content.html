DealScan contains detailed deal terms from March 31, 2010 back to 1988, and selected data back to 1981. It contains extensive and reliable information on the global commercial loan market. It provides users with access to Loan Pricing Corporation's (LPC's) robust database of detailed terms and conditions on over 131,000 loan, high-yield bond and private placement transactions.

Access to the DealScan database and associates materials is through download from the University of Alberta Libraries institutional repository (ERA). PLEASE NOTE: The DealScan DataFeed is a Microsoft Access database so you must have Microsoft Access installed on your computer in order to use it. In addition, the file is very large and may take half an hour to download.
