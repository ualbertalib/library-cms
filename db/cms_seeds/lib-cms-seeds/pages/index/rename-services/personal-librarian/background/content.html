Background of the Program

The University of Alberta Libraries are excited to offer a support service called the Personal Librarian. For the 2014-2015 and 2015-2016 school years this initiative was directed towards self-identified first-year Aboriginal/FNMI Students. In 2016-2017, the program expanded to include first-year international students. Watch here for more information as the project continues to develop!

For further information about the Personal Librarian program, please email personal.librarian@ualberta.ca
