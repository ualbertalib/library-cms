Personal Librarians at the University of Alberta

Each self-identified first-year Aboriginal student or a first-year international student at the University of Alberta will be matched with one of the Personal Librarians below. If you are not sure whether you have a Personal Librarian, or who your Personal Librarian is, please contact personal.librarian@ualberta.ca to find out.
