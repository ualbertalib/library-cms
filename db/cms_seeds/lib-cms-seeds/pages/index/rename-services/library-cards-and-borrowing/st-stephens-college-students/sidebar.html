Library Cards - St. Stephen's College Students

Library cards can be obtained at the Service Desk in the Rutherford Humanities & Social Sciences Library. Photo ID will be required.

To keep track of your library materials, holds, and fines, please use My Account.

Access to U of A Libraries Online Resources

St. Stephen's College students may access library licensed online resources on-site only. To access online resources via library computer terminals or via the U of Alberta wireless network, obtain a Temporary Network ID by presenting your photo ID with proof of a Canadian address at any library Service Desk.
For more info see: Temporary Network IDs.

Library Privileges & Responsibilities

Use of the University Library is a privilege with associated responsibilities for all clients. Within the parameters of the policies, no user should be unduly disadvantaged through the use or abuse of the library by another client, whatever the client group.
