NEOS Cards

Those affiliated with a NEOS member library, may borrow materials from the University of Alberta Libraries collection using a library card issued by their home library.

To keep track of your library materials, holds, and fines, please use My Account.

Access to Online Resources

NEOS visitors to U of Alberta Libraries may access library licensed online resources on-site only. To access online resources via library computer terminals or via the U of Alberta wireless network, obtain a Temporary Network ID by presenting your NEOS library card at any library Service Desk.

For more info see: Temporary Network IDs.

Library Privileges & Responsibilities

Use of the University Library is a privilege with associated responsibilities for all clients. Within the parameters of the policies, no user should be unduly disadvantaged through the use or abuse of the library by another client, whatever the client group.
