Already Registered? Place Requests

To register for lending services via web form, please contact us to obtain a User ID and Password. You will need to provide the following information:

  • Institution and Library Name
  • AMICUS or OCLC Symbol
  • Mailing Address
  • Billing Address
  • Preferred Delivery Method and Delivery Information (Email/Odyssey/Other)
  • Contact Information

Before requesting an account, please review our Fee Schedule.

Delivery Options

  • Post-to-web - Link is sent in an email where you click to download the document.
  • Email - Document is sent as an attachment. (Make sure you can accommodate larger file sizes.)
  • Mail - Book or paper copy is sent via Canada Post unless otherwise specified (Purolator is our preferred courier).

Note: We don't support fax delivery.

Borrowing from the University of Alberta Libraries

Our ILL symbol in Canada: AEU (click for detailed information)

We accept the following methods to send requests to us:

  1. ILL system (e.g. Relais, VDX)
  2. Web form (see Place a Request on the right)
  3. OCLC: UAB
  4. Docline: ABCUJW

We do not accept requests through e-mail, over the phone, or via fax.

Rush requests must be confirmed by calling our office (780) 492-3795.
