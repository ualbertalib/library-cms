AtoM

This digital archives project will facilitate searching archival records and collections at the University of Alberta by replacing paper finding aids for archival content. This project will require content migration and the linking of digital content from a variety of sources to be made discoverable through the open source software AtoM (Access to Memory).

The University of Alberta's AtoM is aimed to be publicly available for winter 2017, with content updated and added continually.

The AtoM project can be viewed online at https://discoverarchives.library.ualberta.ca/index.php/.
