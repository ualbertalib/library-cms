Avalon Media System

The Avalon Media System is a robust, fully open source solution for curating and providing online access to digital audio and video collections in libraries and archives. Built on major open source technologies including Hydra, Fedora, Solr, Blacklight, and Opencast Matterhorn. Avalon is being developed collaboratively by several high-profile institutional partners, including the libraries at Indiana University and Northwestern University. The University of Alberta Libraries are using Avalon to support a media streaming service, ERA A+V, for the University of Alberta community, and will be working to accommodate future integration with the Universityâ€™s well-established institutional repository, ERA: Education and Research Archive.
