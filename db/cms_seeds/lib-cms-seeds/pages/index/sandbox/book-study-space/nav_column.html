Rules

  • available to University of Alberta students, faculty and staff only.
  • A group (not each member of a group) may book one room for one block per day.
  • Minimum group size is 2 people.
  • Maximum time block = 2.5 hrs ( except for Cameron Library = 2 hrs)
  • Rooms can be booked up to seven days in advance.
  • delete your booking if you are unable to attend (Note: A 10 minute grace period will be given for all room bookings and occupancy, after which time groups may forfeit their booking to other groups who may be waiting for a space to open.)
  • To ensure equitable access to Cameron Library Group Study Rooms during exam periods, rules may be revised. Access to Group Study Rooms is to be shared by all students.Students are reminded that block booking of rooms is not acceptable.
  • Groups that book using inappropriate language will have their bookings deleted. Code of Student Behaviour

Study Room Use

  • Rooms are not secure. You are responsible for all personal items left in your group room.
  • Please remove all personal items and garbage when vacating rooms.
  • take care of the equipment and facilities so that we can continue to offer you these services.
