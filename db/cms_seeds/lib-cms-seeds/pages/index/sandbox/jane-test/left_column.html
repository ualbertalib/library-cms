<h2>Equipment Lending</h2>

<p>Our Equipment Lending Program is a service for University of Alberta students and staff.</p>

Link to equipment and peripherals

<p><em>Users should be aware that the <strong>laptops are not encrypted</strong>, and must not be used to create, store, or access any university sensitive information. Please see <a href="#Liability">Liability and Personal Information</a> below.</em></p>

All personal information, including files and saved documents, is deleted when the laptop is restarted.
 Please save your work to a USB stick, your AFS space or other Cloud services.


<h2>Borrowing Privileges</h2>

<p>The equipment lending program is designed for short-term, individual use. We cannot privilege individual borrowers by allowing longer loans, or by putting equipment aside for specific people or classes.<br><br>

<strong>Equipment and all related peripherals must be returned to the service desk of their home library during Service Desk <a data-no-turbolink="true" href="/hours" target="_blank">opening hours</a>. Service desks are not open during extended study hours at Cameron and Rutherford Libraries.</strong></p>

<ul>

<li>Laptops, Cameras, Tripods: 48 hr loan, $5/hour per overdue item.</li>

<li>Peripherals lent with laptops (cords, bags, mice): 48 hour loan, $1/hour per overdue item.</li>

<li>Digital Voice Recorders: 48 hour loan, $1/hour per overdue item.</li>

<li>Standalone Peripherals (chargers, phone cords, headphones, etc.): 4 hr loan, $1/hour per overdue item. Borrowers are responsible for matching standalone peripherals, such as chargers, to their personal devices.</li>

</ul>

<p>Borrowers are subject to the University of Alberta Libraries <a data-no-turbolink="true" href="/services/library-cards-and-borrowing/undergraduate-students">borrowing policies</a>.<br>

Avoid fines. Return on time. No renewals. No holds.</p>

<p>Replacement charges:</p>

<ul>

<li>Digital camera: $2,500</li>

<li>Mac laptop: $1,500</li>

<li>PC laptop: $1,000</li>

<li>Canon Power-Shot Camera: $900</li>

<li>Tripod: $500</li>

<li>Digital Voice Recorder: $80</li>

<li>Peripherals: varies ($20-$100)</li>

<li>Damaged equipment will be assessed to determine what fees will apply</li>

</ul>

<p>Please note: replacement charges will be applied in place of the $150 lost charge that is indicated in your library account.</p>

<h2 id="Liability">Liability and Personal Information</h2>

All personal information, including files and saved documents, is deleted when the laptop is restarted. Please save your work to a USB stick, your AFS space or other Cloud services. Memory from other equipment will be formatted and cleared of data upon returning. The Library accepts no responsibility for any loss or damage to user files.

The laptops are not encrypted and in accordance with the Information Technology Security Policy and Encryption Procedure must not be used to create, store or access any university sensitive information. Similarly, your own personal information, data or actions are not encrypted. The Library accepts no responsibility for unauthorized access and disclosure of university sensitive information. For information on what constitutes sensitive information, please view the University of Alberta's Encryption Procedure. For more information on mobile computing security, please visit Best Practices for Securing University Information on Mobile Devices page.

<p><em></em></p>

The use of the borrowed equipment is subject to the University of Alberta’s Code of Student Behaviour and Information technology Use and Management Policy. Students assume full responsibility and financial liability for the borrowed equipment and their peripherals.
