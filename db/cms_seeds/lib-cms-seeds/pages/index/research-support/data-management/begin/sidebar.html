Tools to Organize my Data

DMP ASSISTANT

DMP is a Library supported, web-based tool that guides you through developing your Data Management Plan (DMP). DMP builder is accessible by UA faculty and students and those beyond our institution.

DATAVERSE

Dataverse is a library supported, web-based tool to help you protect and share your research data. Dataverse provides remote, secure backup of your research data. Dataverse also allows you to share your data with specific individuals. You can set access permissions for your data so your research team or students can use it. Dataverse allows you to maintain versions of files as the data undergoes analysis and revision.
