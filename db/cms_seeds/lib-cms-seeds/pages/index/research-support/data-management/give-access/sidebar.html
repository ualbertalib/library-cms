Tools for Sharing Data

DATAVERSE

Dataverse is one way to share copies of your data (and keep previous versions). You have the ability to set permissions for your data so members on a research team or students can use it or you can share a copy with the world.

Dataverse system issues your file a persistent identifier (known as a DOI) upon upload. The DOI is registered with DataCite. DataCite allows discovery of your data through a variety of search tools.

DOI's provides the persistent link that can be published with your related research articles this encourages reuse and data citation, which adds to your research profile.
