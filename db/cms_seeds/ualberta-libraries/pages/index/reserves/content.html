Borrowing Reserve Readings

  • Reserves cannot be renewed
  • Borrowing periods: 2 hours (some exceptions)
  • Borrow within two hours of closing and materials are due at library opening the next day.
  • Reserves must be returned to the library from which they were borrowed.
  • Overdue fine: $1.00/hr (includes closed library hours)

Self-Serve Reserve at Rutherford, Cameron, and Coutts:

  • You must check in items to the same self-serve machine you used to borrow.
  • Return reserve items at a Rutherford, Cameron, and Coutts book drop only when libraries are closed.

Questions? Email Us: reserves@ualberta.ca
