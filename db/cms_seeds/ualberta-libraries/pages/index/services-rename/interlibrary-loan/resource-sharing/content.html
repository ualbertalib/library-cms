Guaranteed turnaround time!

We provide an expedited service that guarantees same day shipment of requested items.

Requests received by 8:00 a.m. will be retrieved and shipped the same day.* Material is either delivered electronically or shipped by commercial courier, providing exceptional turnaround times for your users.
