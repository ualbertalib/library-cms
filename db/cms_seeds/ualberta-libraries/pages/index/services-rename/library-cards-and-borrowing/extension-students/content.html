Borrowing Privileges - Extension Students

Extension students enrolled in a degree/diploma program or English Language School (formerly known as English Language Program - ELP) - may obtain a ONEcard from the ONEcard Office and bring it to the library to be registered for library services. Extension students enrolled in a degree/diploma program or in English Language School have the same borrowing privileges as U of A students

Extension students in all other courses may get a Public Borrower or Alberta Library Card.
